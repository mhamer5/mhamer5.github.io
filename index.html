I am a junior entomological researcher, with a particular interest in myrmecology alongside insect diversity, faunistics as well as taxonomy and evolution. Currently based at the University of Hong Kong as a research assistant within the Insect Biodiversity and Biogeography Laboratory investigating exotic and tramp insect species in and around Hong Kong city.

Photography

Alongside research, I am an avid macro photographer with a particular interest capturing biodiversity through my lens - both my research and photography are therefore often intertwined. I publish all my photographs formally onto flickr.
