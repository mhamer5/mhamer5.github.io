I am a junior entomological researcher, with a particular interest in myrmecology alongside insect diversity, faunistics as well as taxonomy and evolution. Currently based at the University of Hong Kong as a research assistant within the Insect Biodiversity and Biogeography Laboratory investigating the ants of Hong Kong as well as other projects in the lab.
